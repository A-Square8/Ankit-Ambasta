Restaurant Billing Solution

I developed a robust restaurant bill counter management system to streamline billing processes. Using MySQL for database management and PHP for backend logic, the system integrates seamlessly to handle various aspects of restaurant operations. It features a user-friendly interface for quick and accurate order entry, bill generation, and payment processing. The system supports menu updates, inventory management, and sales reporting, automating tasks to reduce errors and improve efficiency. This solution enhances operational workflow and provides a smoother experience for both staff and customers.

Password Manager

I developed a Python-based password manager that ensures secure handling of user credentials. Using the cryptography library, I implemented AES encryption and password hashing to protect sensitive data. The application features a command-line interface for user registration, password storage, and management, with strong password validation. This project highlights my skills in Python and cryptographic techniques, demonstrating my ability to build secure and functional applications.

About Me

Born in Pune and with roots tracing back to Munger in Bihar, my journey through life has been marked by a series of relocations due to my father's dynamic profession. This constant change exposed me to a rich variety of cultures and environments, shaping my worldview significantly. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the scenic beauty of Srinagar, each city contributed uniquely to my understanding of the world. Hashimara, a small town on the outskirts of West Bengal, offered a close-knit community feel, while the historical city of Agra left a lasting impression with its architectural wonders and vibrant local culture. Eventually, we settled in Vadodara, a city that became a new home and provided a sense of stability.

These diverse environments instilled in me the values of adaptability and appreciation for different lifestyles. The constant exposure to new places taught me to embrace change and see the richness in varying cultural experiences. My educational journey has mirrored this eclectic upbringing. I attended several schools across India, each offering its own set of experiences and opportunities. Air Force School Agra, where I completed my schooling, holds a special place in my heart. It was here that my interest in computers first sparked, marking the beginning of my journey into technology.

Currently, as I pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science at VIT Vellore, I reflect on how these varied experiences have shaped my academic and personal growth. The contrast between the historical allure of Agra and the modern, tech-focused environment at VIT serves as a constant reminder of the multifaceted nature of life. This juxtaposition fuels my passion for technology and motivates me to integrate diverse perspectives into my work.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I strive to maintain a balanced lifestyle. Sports play a significant role in my life; whether it's basketball or cricket, I enjoy the thrill and camaraderie that these activities provide. Indoors, I engage in strategic games like chess and carromboard, which offer a mental challenge and a different kind of stimulation.

In addition to sports and technology, literature and cinema are crucial aspects of my life. I am an avid reader with a particular interest in political intrigue, post-apocalyptic settings, and psychological dramas. Books and movies offer me a way to explore complex human emotions and societal dynamics, enriching my understanding of the world and complementing my technical interests.
